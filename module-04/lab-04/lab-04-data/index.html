Cuzco is the ancient power base of the former Incan Empire which once spanned the South American continent from present-day Colombia to what is now northern Argentina. Cuzco is now the key transportation hub in the Peruvian Andes and a major tourist attraction.

Tokyo became the capital of Imperial Japan in 1869 after the Meiji Emperor moved it from Kyoto. The city is now the second largest metropolitan area in the world and is a global center for business, finance and industry.

Tromso is the most populous city located beyond the Arctic Circle and is the gateway to the Lofoten Islands and to the island of Svalbard.

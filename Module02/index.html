What we have here is an ugly map of the world for MAP 672 class!! But I really should comment on the Pickles' excerpt. The map image seen below (while albeit ugly) is a rudimentary representation of Pickles' all-encompassing world-eye view. Maps have their power in their capacity to lie. Note how the eye starts at the red colored pixels at the top and descend to the plain colored pixels at the bottom, thus creating a Northern Hemipshere-superior world model.
